Hall of Heroes

Fargrim of Isiliath

Fargrim helped to maintain peace in Breskoll and rebuild the city's recently burned-down cathedral. However, when reports of rebellion started coming in from Eolund, Fargrim was ordered to lead an army of Freeholders into Eolund to help topple the province's Marquis. After pledging himself to Selune, the goddess of twilight, in the Feywild, Fargrim has returned to the Prime to lead his army to victory!

Orryn Reese

Having been slain by a beholder in the otherworldy bodega known only as the Pressed Rat and Warthog, the Gnome Ranger made peace with his wife before the Gnomish Pantheon sent his soul back to the Prime. Reinvigorated with new life and purpose, Orryn Reese seeks to uncover the sinister threat to his species before its machinations lead to the extinction of the gnomish peoples!

Zeed of Aaqa

An eerie and ultimately violent encounter with the Coven of Prophecy in the cursed wood of the Feywild haunts Zeed as the witches seemed to think he was a prophesized Chosen One. With the witches seemingly defeated, Zeed hopes to unravel the mystery of his fate and what role the cursed storm giantess Angrboda will play in it.

Johnboy Tealeaf

Since the party's abrupt and vengeful exile from Bummdom on account of the Marquis of Eolund's death, Johnboy has spent the many months on the road in both the Prime and the Feywild longing to return to his newly beloved Cora Kettlewhistle in Westhoek. Now back in his native plane and on the eve of battle for that very town, Johnboy hopes to sneak past the guards to embrace his love once more!

Hothor Shatterstone

Rescued from the Dryskull orcs, the eldritch knight Hothor is Dovahkiin, an ancient line of dragonslayers from the western realms. Upon meeting Fargrim and his army, Hothor became cursed when a cultist of the Demogorgon lured him into a trap. After Fargrim restored his fellow dwarf's mind in the Feywild, Hothor returned to the Prime with the mythical Spear of Lugh to see his vengeance fulfilled.
