The Good Party

Having eliminated the Crimson Brotherhood in glorious battle, the Good Party is sneaking into Westhoek after a finch informed Orryn that the army's scout's have been captured and will be executed. With only 24 hours to spare, our heroes wasted no time in sneaking into the heavily manned motte-and-bailey village of Westhoek. With great cunning and magic, the party disguised Zeed as a fire-breathing dragon to distract the guards while the others slipped by. After consuming several potions of growth and receiving Hothor's fire breath, Zeed took to the skies and caused utter havoc and panic among the poor souls watching the walls of Westhoek that night. As Hothor, Orryn, and Fargrim hasten to the shirrif's holding cells to rescue the captured scouts, Johnboy raced to home of his beloved Cora Kettlewhistle. After many long months in the Feywild, our halfling champion has longed to be with the lovely jeweller once more. Will Johnboy reunite with his love? Will the party save the scouts and uncover the plots of evil's forces before it's too late? Find out next time in The Rise of Valac!

The Evil Party

With their minds shattered, the Evil Party feels compelled to investigate the Dreamfort of Sokahlsh. As our villains neared this sinister fortress carved from a massive stone crag, the members of the Evil Party couldn't help the feeling as if they were being watched. When they arrived at the great stone door guarded by ogre zombies, the servants of Paimon were greeted by a strange red "eye" in the door. Koriand'r was able to understand the ominous entity and negotiate with it, yet In her ignorance, the sorceress insulted the entity with her "gift" of slaves. When Koriand'r resisted the telekinetic force meant to vanquish her, the mysterious outcast Bardis surprised everyone by taunting the entity in its native tongue. Maximus Bardis' hubris was short lived as the furious creature disintegrated the eldritch warlock on the spot. Taking their cue, the worshippers of Paimon quickly slew the guards and made their way into the darkness of the citadel. What terrors await our villains within the Dreamfort of Sokahlsh? Who will die next? Find out next time in The Rise of Valac!
